About Us

We want to inspire change

Here at GetHealthy, we hope to inspire individuals to start living a healthier lifestyle. The journey to getting healthier is a very daunting task at the beginning. We want to help show you just how easy you can start living a much healthier lifestyle. By incorperating working out and eating healthy (yet delicious!) meals, you too can begin your journey to living the lifestyle of your dreams!

What You Will Learn

Learn From Our Experiences

Here at GetHealthy, we want you to be able to gain the insight neccessary to avoid the "rookie mistakes" that come with first getting into shape. We will introduce you to three unique workout routines; all the way from beginner to an experienced level. On top of this, we will also introduce to fundamental nutrition concepts for individuals that are both looking to gain muscle mass or get leaner.

About Me

Who Am I?

My name is Kellen Bavis and I am currently a Senior at the University at Albany. I was inspired to begin my fitness journey my Freshman Year of College. I was a scrawny, 110lb young man coming into school. I decided that it was time to start making changes in my life. With the workout routines and nutrition plans that I will be sharing, I was able to gain 50 lbs of lean muscle. While altering my daily routine to be more healthy, the positive impact it has had on my life outside of this journey is immeasurable.
