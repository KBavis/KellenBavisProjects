Plan for Bulking

Image Reference

Sustained calorie surplus to gain muscle mass

Bulking is a method in which you are eating a calorie surplus (eating more calories than your burning) in order to gain weight in the form of muscle or fat. While eating a calorie surplus, you are able to train more intense which allows for you to gain muscle and increase your strength.

Following determing the number of calories YOU need to maintain your current weight, you should ensure that your eating around a 10-20% calorie surplus (Reference).

Foods to include in your diet:

  • Lean proteins (chicken/fish)
  • Healthy fats (olive oil, nuts, seeds)
  • High quality carbs (oats, whole grain pasta)
  • Fruits (apples, all types of berries)
  • And more!

Plan for Cutting

Image Reference

Loosing weight while retaining muscle mass

Cutting, also known as shredding, is done with the intent to lose fat while maintaing muscles. Cutting is NOT the same for you as it is for everyone else. However, there are some general principles that you should aim to acheive through your process.

In general you should consume around 2.8 grams of protein per kilogram of bodyweight (roughly 25-30% of total calorie intake). 15-30% of your total calories should be fat, and the rest (around 55-60%) should be carbohydrates. (Reference)

Foods to include in your diet:

  • Lean meats(chicken,fish)
  • Eggs
  • Milk and yogurt
  • Leafy greens
  • And more!

My Favorite Meal

Image Reference

Who Doesn't Love a Macro Friendly Meal!

My favorite meal to make is a Creamy Buffalo Chicken Grilled Cheese. Here's how you make it!

  1. Chop up some parsley and some chives.
  2. Season 16oz of chicken thighs with your choice of seasoning (I usually do some garlic salt, pepper, onion powder, and buffalo seasoning).
  3. Cook chicken thighs on high heat until cooked through
  4. Create the creamy sauce by blending the following: 100g reduced fat cheddar cheese, 60g 1/3 cream cheese, some garlic paste, and buffalo sauce
  5. Mix in your creamy sauced with your cooked chicken in a pan
  6. Toast 2 whole grain slices of bread with your creamy chicken on one of the slices and enjoy!
