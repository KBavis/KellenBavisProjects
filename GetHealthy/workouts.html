Workout Plans

Beginner Workout Plan

Image Reference

3 Sets of 10 Reps Each (Push, Pull, Legs)
  • Day 1: Dumbell Bench Press, Dumbell Shoulder Press, Tricep Push Downs
  • Day 2: Lat Pull Downs, Curls, Seated Rows
  • Day 3: Barbell Back Squat, Dumbell Lunges, Leg Extensions

Intermediate Workout Plan

Image Reference

3 Sets of 10 Reps Each (Push, Pull, Legs)
  • Day 1: Barbell Bench Press, Barbell Shoulder Press, Tricep Kick-Backs, Incline Dumbell Chest Press, Lateral Raises, Overhead Dumbell Tricep Extensions
  • Day 2: Preacher Curls, Hammer Curls, Dumbell Rows, Cable Lat Pullover, Assited Pull Ups
  • Day 3: Barbell Back Squat, Barbell Lunges, Hamstring Curls, Dumbell Calf Raises

Expert Workout Plan

Image Reference

3 Sets of 10 Reps Each (Push, Pull, Legs)
  • Day 1: Barbell Bench Press, Arnold Shoulder Press, Skull-Crushers, Barbell Incline Press, Dumbell Front Raises, Dumbell Lateral Raises, Cable Flys, Dips, Tricep Rope Press Downs
  • Day 2: Lat Pull Downs, Pull-Ups, Bent-Over Row, Cable Lat Pullovers, Seated Incline Dumbell Curls, Hammer Curls, Preacher Curls
  • Day 3: Barbell Back Squat, Romanian Dead Lift, Leg Extensions, Bulgarian Split-Squat, Calf Raises
